I’m excited to share my latest project: an interactive Udemy Performance Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel

Understanding what behind the data is key to making informed business decisions. For this project, I analyzed Udemy course data from 2011–2017 to identify key growth drivers and market patterns.

Key Strategic Insights

- Top-Performing Categories: Identified that Web Development is the primary revenue driver, significantly outperforming other subjects in both subscribers and review volume.

- Growth Trends: Pinpointed a major surge in content production between 2014–2016, which directly correlated with peak revenue periods.

- Market Preference: Discovered that beginner-level courses hold the highest volume of students, highlighting a strong market demand for foundational skill acquisition.

- Drill-Down Capability: Built interactive slicers to allow stakeholders to filter performance by subject, difficulty, and year for real-time reporting.

Technical Toolkit
 - Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers)
 - Data Cleaning & Transformation
 - Business Reporting & Visualization

This project helped me refine my ability to turn raw datasets into a narrative that supports strategic decision-making.
